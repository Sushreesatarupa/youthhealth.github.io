HIV or AIDs (Acquired Immuno Deficiency Syndrome)

It means deficiency of immune system acquired during the life time of an individual indicating that it is not a congenital diseases. It was first reported in 1981 and till now it has spread all over the world killing more than 25 million people. It is a viral diseases caused by HIV , a member of retro virus that decreases the no. of helper T Cells in the body making the immune system weak.

What is “Reproductive Health?

It is a state of complete physical, mental & social wellbeing and not merely the absence of disease or infirmity, in all matters relating to the reproduction system and its functions and processes.

• They can be inserted into the vagina upto eight hours before sex. It prevents pregnancy and STDs by not allowing sperm entry. They are 95% effective.

• It is a pill iused to prevent pregnancy. It contains hormones that block the release of eggs from the ovaries; mostly estrogen and progesterone. It is 93% effective.

• Theses releases the hormones progesterone into the blood stream which helps in preventing pregnancy. It is 97% effective.

• It is a small flexible plastic rod that is placed under the skin in upper arm. It releases progesterone to prevent pregnancy and lasts for 3 years.
